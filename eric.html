ERIC FENG

Ninja

Eric Feng is currently the Chief Technology Officer at Flipboard, a personal magazine used by more than 80MM people. Previously Eric was the founder and CEO of Erly, a social startup funded by Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers (where Eric was also a Partner) and acquired in 2012. Before joining Kleiner Perkins, Eric was the founding CTO at Hulu, a joint venture of NBC Universal, News Corp. and The Walt Disney Company.
